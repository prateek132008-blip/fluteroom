Fees

The monthly fee for live one-on-one flute classes is ₹1499, covering unlimited classes as described below.

What "unlimited" means

"Unlimited" means a student can request the next session once the assigned topic or task has been completed and practiced. It does not mean unlimited class hours in a single day, and there is no fixed weekly schedule.

Scheduling

  • Classes are scheduled within the available daily slots: 3 PM – 6 PM and 9 PM – 12 AM.
  • Students should inform the teacher in advance if they are unable to attend a scheduled class.
  • The teacher may reschedule a class due to unavoidable circumstances.

Fees and refunds

Fees are non-refundable once the monthly class cycle begins, except where required by applicable law.

Enrollment confirmation

Enrollment is confirmed only after the teacher has manually verified your payment screenshot sent on WhatsApp.

Course materials

Curriculum documents, recordings, and other course materials shared with students are for personal learning use only and may not be redistributed or resold without the teacher's written permission.
