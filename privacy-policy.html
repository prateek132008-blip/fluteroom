Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DD Month YYYY]

This policy explains what information The Flute Room collects when you visit this website or enroll in a class, and how that information is used.

Information we collect

  • Full name
  • WhatsApp number
  • Email address
  • Current flute-playing level
  • Whether you own a flute (and, if not, your budget preference)
  • Your intended start time
  • Any optional message or learning goal you share

Why we collect it

Your information is used only to:

  • Process and manage your class enrollment
  • Communicate with you about your classes over WhatsApp or email
  • Verify your payment and confirm your seat
  • Provide support when you have questions

How your data is handled

Your information is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the teacher, Prateek Jha. We do not sell your information to any third party.

Analytics and advertising

This website uses the Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Pixel and standard website analytics to understand how visitors use the site and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These tools may collect limited information about your browser or device activity on this site. You can control this through your browser or device privacy settings.

Payments

Payments are made manually through UPI/QR code, and verified by the teacher after you share a screenshot on WhatsApp. We do not store your banking or UPI credentials.

Contact us

For any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at prateek132008@gmail.com or on WhatsApp.
