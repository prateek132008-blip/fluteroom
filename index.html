The curriculum

What you will learn

A step-by-step path from your very first breath on the flute to full songs, taught at a pace that suits you.

Beginner Foundations

First sound to first scale

Blowing technique, posture, holding the flute, clean sound, sargam, octaves, alankars, rhythm, scales and tanpura.

Technique & Expression

Ornamentation & control

Komal swars, chromatic scale, meend, gamak, kan swars, murki, khatka, zamzamma and tonguing.

Songs & Application

Music you'll actually play

Bollywood songs, devotional bhajans, popular flute tunes, sound control, expression, scale shifting and modulation.
