Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to learn flute?▼

Absolutely not! Flute Room welcomes complete beginners of Age 12+. Our curriculum is designed to start from the very basics — including how to hold the flute and produce your first note.

What type of flute do I need to buy?▼

You'll need a basic bamboo bansuri flute (Scale C or B♭ recommended for beginners). We'll guide you on exactly which flute to purchase before your first class — typically available for ₹300–₹2200 online or at local music stores.

Is the demo class really free? Any hidden charges?▼

Yes, 100% free. No hidden charges, no credit card required. The 15-minute demo is purely for you to experience the teaching style and decide if it's the right fit for you.

What happens after the free demo?▼

After your demo, if you'd like to continue, you can subscribe to our monthly plan for ₹1,499/month which includes Unlimited Live classes. There's no pressure — enroll only if you love it!

What is Unlimited Live Classes?▼

♾ Unlimited Classes – Learn at Your Pace • Each class = 1 task/topic • Practice on your own time • Once ready, message or call to book your next class (even the same day if available) 📩 You can also send your practice video for feedback — I’ll guide you and move you to the next step. ➡️ The more you practice, the faster you progress 🎯

What are the class timings?▼

✨ *We offer flexible class timings to fit your schedule!* 🕙 *_Available class times:_* 🕞 EVENING SLOTS - 3:00 PM | 4:00 PM | 5:00 PM 🌙 NIGHT SLOTS - 9:00 PM | 10:00 PM | 11:00 PM 👉 *You can choose your class time before every session as per your schedule (_subject to slot availability_).* This broad window accommodates students across India and international time zones.

Can I reschedule or cancel a class?▼

Yes. You can reschedule any class at least 1 hours before the scheduled time via WhatsApp. We understand life gets busy and offer flexible rescheduling within the same month.
